The Gameplay

VALORANT is a tactical first-person shooter filled with action and thrills, it has taken the gaming industry by storm. The creators of the infamous game named League of Legends, Riot Games, developed it. This game combines strategy along precise gunplay, creating an experience that is both very rewarding and challenging.

In depth take

Pros of the Community

VALORANT features a lot of good sides that helped its rise into the gaming world and the most important one is its dedication towards its community. From beginners and casual players to professional esports teams, this game has attracted players of all skill levels. It is easy to notice teamwork among players, making it easy to find friends to team up with and share amazing moments.

Cons of the Community

Like any other online gaming community, VALORANT is no exception when it comes to toxic and inappropriate behavior during game sessions. Players can be very competitive that it leads them to output unnecessary unkind comments, so it is very important to keep a light mind towards mean behavior and pressing the mute and report functions built in the game when necessary.

Learning Curve Can Be Challenging

VALORANT’s learning curve can be quite rough on beginners, mainly newcomers into the tactical shooter genre. Becoming very good at VALORANT in one night is not something that we should expect when we start playing. This game contains many complex elements to learn, it has agent abilities, map layouts, and teamwork. All of that takes time to practice and learn. Noticing your improvement in these games is very satisfying, so if you’re willing to invest the effort, the journey is definitely rewarding.

Audience

VALORANT is a game that meets the criterias of a lot of players, therefore leading it to obtain such an immense audience. Whether you’re a competitive gamer looking for “try hard” gameplay, a casual player seeking some fun sessions with your friends, or a content creator looking to entertain your viewers and audience, VALORANT has you covered.

Overall Experience

I’ve been following and playing this game since the closed-beta release. Four eventful years, filled with amazing acquaintances and laughter mixed with rage towards this game. Valorant taught me how to stay disciplined and patient no matter how stressful the situation can get. It also proved to me that learning and getting better takes time. Whether you are a sweaty competitive gamer, a casual player or a content creator, Valorant will always find a way to suit your liking.
